History of The Ghosbusters and the begining of GhostCorps


Three misfit parapsychology research professors that specialize in ghosts, Dr. Egon Spengler, Dr. Raymond Stantz, and Dr. Peter Venkman, find themselves looking for work after Columbia University terminates their grant. Before they are kicked out of Columbia, they investigate a haunting at the New York City Public Library and after seeing some symmetrical book stacking they encounter a ghost librarian (the Gray Lady) that runs them out of the Library.

Without a way to share their findings, Venkman, Stantz, and Spengler take matters into their own hands. They start a business named "Ghostbusters", a "professional paranormal investigation and elimination service", out of an old firehouse, using a 1959 Cadillac Miller-Meteor Ambulance dubbed "Ecto-1" to get about the city and hiring Janine Melnitz to handle the phones and clerical work.

Meanwhile, their funds are nearly dried up eating a Chinese food dinner, which they ate slowly. Janine gets a call with a serious client, and she rings the alarm bell. The Ghostbusters run and get dressed, then leave in the Ecto-1. They show up at the Sedgewick Hotel and the Hotel manager tells them that they are having problems with a resident ghost.

Following a successful test of the equipment, they split up to search the hotel for the ghost. Peter finds the ghost which then slimes him, their first real encounter. After they track the ghost to the ballroom in the hotel, they attempt to capture it and in process they destroy the room. They ultimately manage to capture the ghost, and they find themselves an overnight success across both New York City and the nation. As the amount of calls grows, the team is required to hire a fourth member, Winston Zeddemore.

After years of success, they team was split up when Dr. Ego Spangler left the team and New York City, taking most of the teams equipment with him. Sadly, shortly after leaving Dr. Spangler passed away while investigating a reported haunting in Summerville Oklahoma. It was after this point that the team was reunited and GhostCorps was formed, now having Ghostbusters in every city and continent to assist and investigate all maters of the supernatural.
